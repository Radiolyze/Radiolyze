# Glossary

**ASR**: Automatic Speech Recognition — software that converts spoken medical dictation into text.

**DICOM**: Digital Imaging and Communications in Medicine — the international standard for medical imaging data.

**DICOMweb**: HTTP-based API for accessing and exchanging DICOM content (studies, series, instances).

**DICOM SR**: DICOM Structured Report — a standardized format for encoding clinical findings and observations within the DICOM standard.

**MPR**: Multi-Planar Reconstruction — a technique for generating axial, coronal, and sagittal views from volumetric image data.

**QA**: Quality Assurance — automated and manual checks applied to radiology reports before sign-off.

**RAG**: Retrieval Augmented Generation — an AI technique that combines a language model with a retrieval system to ground responses in external knowledge (e.g. clinical guidelines).

**EU AI Act**: European Union regulation on artificial intelligence systems, establishing risk-based requirements for transparency, oversight, and documentation.
